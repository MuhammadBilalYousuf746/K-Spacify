Our Mission

Making event space booking
effortless

Planning any event in Karachi — whether it was a dholki, birthday, corporate meeting, or a cricket match — used to be a hassle. Finding a venue meant physically visiting multiple locations, negotiating rates, and manually confirming availability.

K-Spacify was created to bring everything onto a single platform. Filter options, view details, and send booking requests directly — all from the comfort of your home.

2024

Idea Born

Two students from SZABIST Karachi identified a major gap — there was no proper online listing platform for local venues.

2025

Development Started

Began building the platform using the MEAN stack — Angular frontend, Node.js backend, and MongoDB database.

2025

K-Spacify Launched

Successfully listed venues across multiple areas of Karachi, went live with the booking system, and onboarded our very first users.
